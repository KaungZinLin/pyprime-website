PyPrime

An app to find Prime Numbers and do more stuff.

Download Now

Features

  • 1: Find Prime Numbers between the Given Range (range is given by you and has the integer limit)
  • 2: Find Non-Prime Numbers between the Given Range (range is given by you and has the integer limit)
  • 3: Check if a number is Prime or not
  • 4: Find factors of a Number
